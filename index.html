About

Hello! I am a first-year Ph.D. Student at the Teaching and Learning, Policy and Leadership at the University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Jing Liu. I gained valuable experience as an elementary school teacher in South Korea's public schools and as a data scientist contributing to many projects.

By leveraging the potential of data science, I will explore the untapped complexities and dynamics within and beyond policy arenas and our education system. Below are some keywords related to my current works:

  • Generative AI for Teachers: Balance between personalized and peer-collaborative learning
  • Generative AI, Human Intelligence, and K-12 Education
  • LLMs for Education
  • Computational Social Science
